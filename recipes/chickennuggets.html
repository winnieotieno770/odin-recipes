The Best Crispy Baked Chicken Nuggets

Ingridients

  • 4 skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • olive oil spray

Steps

  1. Season the chicken inside and out.
  2. Add the butter to the chicken and the pan.
  3. Stuff the cavity with celery.
  4. Bake until the chicken is fully roasted.

Chicken nuggets are a real kid-pleaser! Adults will enjoy this recipe for a tasty appetizer, too! Serve warm, with your favorite dipping sauces if desired.
